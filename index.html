I've been online since the 90s. 14.4k dial-up, IRC channels, tracking mods on the Amiga, first website built in Notepad before Netscape even had tabs.

I was early to every major platform, joined them all when they were still empty, before brands, before algorithms.

Now I prefer fewer surfaces.

The last few years I've optimized for minimalism, time efficiency, and focus. Most of my social media is either gone or going.

I'm on X as @willstoneberg. Best place to reach me.

I'm coding apps, games, and whatever else feels worth making. Documenting as I go. Not constantly, not for show, just honestly.

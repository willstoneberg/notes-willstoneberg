[ WHY I'M HERE ]

I've been online since the 90s. 14.4k modems, dial-up tones, IRC channels. I built my first website in the Netscape era.

I was early to every major platform, joined them all when they were still empty, before brands, before algorithms.

Now I prefer fewer surfaces.

The last few years I've optimized for minimalism, time efficiency, and focus. Most of my social media is either gone or going.

This is where I stay. If you want to reach me, this is the place.

I'm building SaaS products, enjoying life, and documenting it here as I go. Not constantly, not for show, just honestly.
